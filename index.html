About

I've been playing disc golf for roughly 12 years now. From the moment I played my first round, I immediately fell in love with the sport. It was through that love that Squalla Disc Golf was born. The Disc Search Tool was the first site we built. We simply just wanted to create a tool that allowed us to quickly and easily find a disc's flight ratings and information. We had alot of fun building this tool and thought it was very useful so we decided to share it with everyone. Ever since then, Squalla DG has been moving forward building tools and apps that improve and / or bring another level up enjoyment to the sport we all already love to play!

Each one of our tools / apps are 100% free to use. Our goal was not to make money. Our goal was to create something awesome and useful.
